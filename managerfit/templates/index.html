How it works

  1. Managers complete a work-style assessment (communication, feedback, autonomy, pace, conflict and more).
  2. Candidates share the manager style they do their best work under.
  3. The fit engine surfaces areas of alignment, areas to discuss, and interview questions for both sides — without ever scoring a manager as "good" or "bad".
